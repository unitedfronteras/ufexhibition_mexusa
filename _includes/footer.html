United Fronteras

The United Fronteras Project brings together active, inactive & in-development works that leverage digital components to document the borderlands from multiple perspectives (literature, archives, art, oral histories, music, among others) from pre-colonial times to the twenty-first century. To learn more about the overall project visit our project homepage.
